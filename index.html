Welcome to Pizza World

Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world. It combines soft dough, delicious sauce, cheese, and many toppings to create a perfect meal.

From its humble beginnings in Italy to becoming a global phenomenon, pizza has captured the hearts and taste buds of people everywhere. Whether you prefer a classic Margherita or a creative modern variation, there's a pizza for everyone.

How To Make a Pizza

Learn the art of pizza making with our step-by-step guide

To make a simple pizza, you need dough, tomato sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings. Spread the sauce, add cheese and toppings, then bake it in the oven.

The key to a great pizza is in the details: a well-risen dough, quality ingredients, and the perfect baking temperature. Don't be afraid to experiment with different combinations to find your personal favorite!

Pizza Types

Margherita Pizza

Hawaiian Pizza

Pizza History

Who Made the First Pizza?

The first modern pizza is believed to have been created in Naples, Italy. Raffaele Esposito is often credited as the person who made the first Margherita pizza.

The story goes that in 1889, Esposito created a pizza featuring tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil to represent the colors of the Italian flag in honor of Queen Margherita of Savoy. This pizza was named "Pizza Margherita" in her honor and remains a classic to this day.
